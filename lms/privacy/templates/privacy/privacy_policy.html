Privacy Policy

Last updated: {{ "now"|date:"F j, Y" }}

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following personal information when you use our library management system:

  • Name and contact information (email, phone number)
  • Library membership details and preferences
  • Borrowing history and book preferences
  • Account activity and login information
  • IP address and technical data for security purposes

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information to:

  • Provide library services and manage your account
  • Process book borrowings and reservations
  • Send important notifications about your account
  • Improve our services and user experience
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud

3. Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate personal data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to Portability: Export your data in a machine-readable format
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to provide our services. Account data is kept for 2 years after account deactivation. Audit logs are retained for 1 year for security purposes.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or wish to exercise your rights, please contact us at privacy@library.com
